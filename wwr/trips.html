Avaliable Trips

  • Beginner's Rapids Adventure

    Perfect for first-timers and families, the Beginner's Rapids Adventure offers a gentle introduction to water rafting. Enjoy the scenic views while navigating through mild rapids with our expert guides.

    • Duration: 3 hours
    • Difficulty Level: Easy
    • Price: $75 per person

    Highlights:

    • Safe and fun for all ages
    • Includes a short training session
    • Complimentary snacks and drinks

  • Intermediate Whitewater Journey

    For those with some rafting experience, the Intermediate Whitewater Journey provides a thrilling adventure through class III and IV rapids. Feel the adrenaline as you conquer the waves and navigate through exciting turns.

    • Duration: 5 hours
    • Difficulty Level: Moderate
    • Price: $120 per person

    Highlights:

    • Professional guides to ensure safety
    • Mid-trip picnic lunch by the riverside
    • Stunning landscapes and wildlife spotting

  • Advanced Rapids Challenge

    Designed for the adventure enthusiasts, the Advanced Rapids Challenge offers an exhilarating experience with class V rapids. This trip is perfect for those seeking a high-intensity rafting adventure.

    • Duration: Full day
    • Difficulty Level: Hard
    • Price: $200 per person

    Highlights:

    • Intense and thrilling rapids
    • Experienced guides with extensive training
    • Includes a gourmet riverside lunch
