About Us White Water Rafting

At White Water Rafting, we offer thrilling water rafting adventures for all skill levels in the beautiful Nile River. Our expert guides ensure a safe and unforgettable experience, whether you're a beginner or an experienced rafter. Join us for an adventure that combines excitement, natural beauty, and lasting memories.

History

Founded in 1985, White Water Rafting began as a small, family-owned business with a passion for the great outdoors and a mission to share the excitement of water rafting with others. Starting with just a few rafts and a team of dedicated enthusiasts, we have grown into one of the most respected rafting companies in Nile River. Our commitment to safety, fun, and environmental stewardship has remained unwavering throughout the years.

Over the decades, we've expanded our offerings and enhanced our services, always striving to provide the best possible experience for our guests. We have introduced a variety of trips catering to different skill levels, from gentle beginner rapids to challenging advanced routes. Our dedication to quality and customer satisfaction has earned us a loyal following and numerous accolades. Today, White Water Rafting is proud to continue our tradition of adventure, welcoming new and returning rafters to explore the wild waters with us.
